# Privacy Policy for Movie Button for IMDb

**Effective Date:** May 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Movie Button for IMDb" browser extension ("the Extension") handles user data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure user experience.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

The Extension **does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, user activity, or browsing history** to any external servers. 

*   **Local Storage:** The Extension uses the browser's local storage (`chrome.storage.local`) solely to store your preferences, such as:
    *   Whether the search buttons (Rezka, Streaming/Database, Torrents) are enabled or disabled.
    *   Your customized lists of preferred search domains, streaming platforms, and torrent trackers.
    *   Your currently selected default search destinations.
    This data remains entirely on your local device and is never sent to us or any third party.
*   **Permissions:**
    *   **Host Permission (`https://www.imdb.com/title/*`):** This is used exclusively to detect when you are on an IMDb movie or TV show page, retrieve the title and release year of the media, and display the shortcut search buttons.
    *   **Storage Permission:** Used only to save your settings locally.

## 2. Information Sharing and Disclosure

Since the Extension does not collect any information, **no user data is shared, sold, rented, or transferred** to any third parties, advertising networks, or analytics services.

## 3. Third-Party Websites

When you click on a custom search button generated by the Extension, you will be redirected to third-party websites of your choice (e.g., search engines, databases, or torrent indexers). We do not control and are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or policies of these external websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites you visit.

## 4. Compliance with Google Developer Policies

We certify that:
*   We do not sell or transfer user data to third parties.
*   We do not use or transfer user data for purposes unrelated to the Extension's single purpose.
*   We do not use or transfer user data to determine creditworthiness or for lending purposes.

## 5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

If you have any questions or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the GitHub repository page.
